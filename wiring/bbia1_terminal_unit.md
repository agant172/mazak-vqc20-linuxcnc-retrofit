Why this board matters
With the Mazatrol NC unit physically removed, this board is now the primary
candidate signal-injection point for the Mesa retrofit. It is a straight
pass-through terminal unit: every top connector (which used to plug into
the NC unit’s back panel, drawing 4143075311/“Control Unit I/O PC’s, Back
Panel FX30”) wires straight through to a corresponding bottom connector
(which goes out to the machine side).
